About JT Trauma Therapy

JT Trauma Therapy provides focused, trauma-informed mental health support for young adults and working professionals. The service prioritises clarity, privacy, and clinical credibility, without call centres or automated systems.

Our Services

What We Offer

  • One-to-one trauma-focused therapy sessions (online and in person)
  • Short-term stabilisation following distressing events
  • Practical tools for anxiety, flashbacks, and sleep disruption
  • Referral letters where clinically appropriate

Who It's For

  • Students managing stress or trauma
  • Young professionals experiencing burnout
  • Individuals recovering from sudden or long-term trauma
  • People seeking private, judgement-free mental health support

Our Therapists

JT Trauma Therapy works with qualified, insured therapists experienced in EMDR, CBT, and integrative counselling. Clients are matched based on individual needs, goals, and availability.
